delete your aura account

aura — the ai that judges your actions and scores your aura

delete in the app (fastest)

  1. open aura and go to your profile
  2. tap settings
  3. scroll to the danger zone at the bottom
  4. tap delete account and confirm

deletion is immediate and permanent. there's no undo.

or request deletion by email

email 1staura@aura-judge.com from the email address on your account with the subject "delete my account". we'll process the deletion within 30 days and confirm when it's done.

what gets deleted

when your account is deleted, we permanently remove:

  • your profile (username, display name, avatar)
  • all your posts, verdicts, and photos
  • your aura balance, tier progress, and gift history
  • your comments and likes
  • your friend connections, blocks, and notifications
  • your push notification tokens and login credentials

what may be kept

content-moderation reports are retained in anonymized form (with your identity removed) to keep the platform safe, as permitted by law. standard server logs age out automatically within a short retention window.
