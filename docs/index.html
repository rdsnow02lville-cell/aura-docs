questions

asked and answered

is aura free?

yes. download it, post, get judged, farm aura. all free.

who sees my posts?

you decide, per post: everyone (the public feed) or just your friends. you can also report and block anyone — the feed stays clean.

is the judge... mean?

the judge is honest. think sarcastic best friend, not internet troll. it'll roast the 2am text and respect the 5am run — sometimes in the same breath.

what's aura actually for?

status, mostly. your score climbs the leaderboard, unlocks tiers, and settles arguments. chaos counts too — a spectacularly bad decision is still content.

when's android?

soon — it's in review on google play right now. ios is live today.
